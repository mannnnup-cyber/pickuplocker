# 1. Purpose

This policy outlines the standard procedures for the collection,
storage, and delivery of customer items to ensure clarity,
accountability, and excellent service.

# 2. Roles & Responsibilities

Production Team:\
- Complete orders according to specifications.\
- Mark orders as \'Ready for Pickup/Delivery\' and hand them over to
Sales.\
- Ensure items are packaged securely before transfer.

Sales Coordinator (Locker & Delivery Lead):\
- Assign lockers for completed orders and issue unique pickup codes to
customers.\
- Communicate pickup instructions via WhatsApp/email.\
- Track locker usage (3 days free, then \$100 JMD/day storage fee).\
- Coordinate delivery requests with third-party couriers.\
- Handle customer inquiries about pickup codes, locker access, and
delivery status.

# 3. Collection Policy

\- Drop-Off: Customers drop off items at the UTech office for
production.\
- Pickup: Completed orders are placed in the locker system. Customers
receive a unique pickup code valid for 3 days. After 3 days, a storage
fee of \$100/day applies until collection.\
- Customers must use the assigned code to access their locker.

# 4. Storage Policy

\- Items awaiting pickup will be stored securely in lockers.\
- Uncollected items after 30 days may be considered abandoned. Dirty
Hand Designs reserves the right to dispose of or resell unclaimed items
after written notice.\
- Locker usage and fees are tracked by the Sales Coordinator.

# 5. Delivery Policy

\- Delivery is available through third-party couriers (e.g., Knutsford,
ZipMail, local courier partners).\
- Customers are responsible for delivery fees, payable in advance.\
- Dirty Hand Designs is not liable for courier delays, damages, or loss
once items leave our premises.\
- Rush/urgent orders may be prioritized but may attract additional
courier or handling fees.

# 6. Customer Responsibility

\- Customers must confirm order details before payment and keep proof of
purchase.\
- Customers are responsible for collecting their items within the pickup
window.\
- If opting for delivery, customers must provide accurate
address/contact details.

# 7. Liability

\- Dirty Hand Designs is responsible for items while in its possession.\
- Once items are placed in the locker (with code issued) or handed to a
courier, Dirty Hand Designs is not liable for loss or damage.
